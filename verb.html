Conjugation of German verb laufen

The conjugation of the verb laufen (run, walk) is irregular. Basic forms are läuft, lief and ist gelaufen. The stem vowels are au - ie - au. The auxiliary verb of laufen is sein. haben can be used as well. The flection is in Active and the use as Main. For a better understanding, countless examples of the verb laufen are available. For practicing and consolidating, there are also free worksheets for laufen. You can not just laufen conjugate, but all German verbs. The verb is part of the thesaurus of Zertifikat Deutsch respectivly Level A1. 1Comment ☆5
